DEO VERITAS SCHOOL

was established in September 2018

The mission of the school is to raise a generation of students/ pupils that are distinguished high standard of academic excellence, lTheadership and character The name "Deo Veritas" is French/Latin meaning "God of Truth" which reflects the place of God as the founder and truth as a core value amongst the six pillars of character which defines the school.

The mission of the school was born out of unquenchable passion to provide quality education which is focused on engaging learners activity by ensuring a conductive environment, instruction and recreational facilities to promote and make learning fun and dynamic

Deo-Verias Schools opened its doors with four (4) pupils on 10 September, 2018; names of pioneer pupils enrolled into basic 1, 2 and 3 in pre-basic with five teachers as pioneers. Since inception, the school's dynamic education environment has continued to evolve and enrollment has grown tremendously.

The e- learning is very good innovation by the Federal Ministry Of Education. It makes teaching and learning simple and enjoyable. It also helps the students to focus and be more involved in the learning process.

This is why DVS has taken the lead in the implementation of the e-learning Program. The school has remained committed to creating an environment in which learners are trained to be bold, confident, independent, outstanding and encouraged to flourish and grow.

The new school building project is a big stride taken by the school management. It adds glamour and more feathers to the achievement cap of the school.
